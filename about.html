Our Story

How it all started

2017: Street-style calisthenics

"We started Start Station right after graduating from NUS and our parents and friends thought we were crazy! We started from scratch, by conducting Calisthenics bootcamps outdoors at different fitness stations across Singapore - from Bedok Reservoir Park to Tiong Bahru Park to Toa Payoh. Over the first few months we got to meet and be inspired by so many different individuals all of different demographics and fitness levels. We love being a part of the fitness journey - being able to witness someone struggling with their planks to finally being able to do a proper push-up and pull-up. That inspires us and gives us the drive to do more and provide more for our students. Every day we keep working towards expanding Start Station and so that everyone will be able to experience bodyweight trainings and eventually fall in love with Calisthenics, Yoga and Movement.‚Äù


2018: Hello indoor studio!

As our community grew, we decided to shift and started conducting our classes indoor in a studio at Core Collective. It was a new experience and the participants loved it!


2019: Our very own sudio!

With the amazing support from our community and friends, we took over what was previously The Yoga Collective studio and finally open up our very own hybrid gym studio. We then rebranded it the the current name - The Faculty of Calisthenics and Yoga. Thank you all for the support!
